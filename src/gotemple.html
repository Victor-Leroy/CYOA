The Enigma of the Eternal Guardian

In a hidden room of the temple,
the adventurer discovers a bas-relief depicting the elements of nature surrounding an empty space in the center,
with an inscription engraved above:

"I am neither animal, nor plant, nor mineral.
I am as old as the world, yet eternally renewed.
I dominate the peaks and carve the valleys, molding the earth in my image.
Without me, life extinguishes.

Who am I?"

Instructions for the player:
Enter the keyword that solves the enigma to reveal the secret guarded by the room.
